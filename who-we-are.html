Arise African Child Foundation is a non-profit charity organization that aims to change the African society’s future through empowerment and mentorship of children particularly those from the poor backgrounds. Arise African Child Foundation was founded in the year 2015 on the 1stof of October. Two young visionaries while still students at Maseno University in Kenya, shared their dreams in a study group and an idea came up to have these dreams merged to achieve greater impact. With the dreams merged; the goal was set to have the African child no longer under the yoke of ignorance of what constantly has become part of their lives. On this basis, the foundation set root in Maseno University, Kisumu-Kenya. AACF recently opened a Nairobi branch which is also the headquarters of the foundation. The guiding principle for AACF has been that the African child shall be exposed to technology and its contents and their stories through this journey told to the world. AACF is therefore looking to offer more than just charity but into going with each step of child development and nurturing the child’s intellectual skills and abilities and talents which are notably seen as from the lower stages of growth of a child’s life.

Mission

Our mission is to provide the African child a highway to success through intellectual skills, knowledge and talent while doing it with great of love

Vision

Our vision is to place the African child on the rightful position in the competitive world
