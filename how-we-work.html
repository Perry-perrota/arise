Empowerment & Charity school programs

The target schools for these programs are schools in poor, marginalized and vulnerable areas, which tend to cater for the grassroot communities. The empowerment programs run for between 3 -6 weeks depending on the school calendar. The program entails empowering either junior or senior classes. Aside from the empowerment programs, school-based charity drives are developed to target groups such as candidates and girls who cannot afford sanitary pads. To achieve the objectives of our projects, we incorporate technology to achieve engagement with the children through digital storytelling. This helps us get a better understanding of a child’s ability and talents.

Empowerment & Charity programs for children's homes, orphanages & rehabilitation centers

The empowerment programs are developed to benefit vulnerable homes. The focus of these programs is to expose and uplift these vulnerable children in these homes. Our approach is to spend close to between 3 – 6 months at the homes.During this period, we execute donation drives to facilitate dry food, stationary and washing stuff as detergents and soap.

Volunteer engagement

We engage university students as volunteers for our empowerment program. Before being engaged in the program they first get trained on empowerment techniques and approaches.
